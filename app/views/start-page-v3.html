C110A Apply for a care, supervision or emergency protection order

Start the process to place a child at risk of harm under the care of the local authority.

You can also apply here for other orders under part 4 of the Children Act 1989.

Start now

Before you start

You’ll need an HM Courts and Tribunals Services (HMCTS) account to apply. Contact HMCTS to request an account.

Other ways to apply

You can also download PDF form and apply by post or email.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
